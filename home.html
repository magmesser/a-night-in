A Night In

These days, there are ways you can enjoy movies without necessarily stepping out to movie theaters. You can now relax and enjoy your movie from the comfort of your own home or yard! Viewing movies can present an awesome opportunity to relieve stress from our lives. It has been medically proven that stress is caused by a continuous buildup of tension within a person and without a way to relieve it, then stress is inevitable. One of the best ways to relieve the buildup of tension is through watching movies.

Now with A Night In, you can search for a meal to pair with your movie for the perfect night in. Search by meal, ingredient, or dietary restrictions (such as vegan, gluten free, or vegetarian). Select your favorite movie genre and let us do the hard part for you -- making decisions! If you like the suggested options, you can select the "I Don't Like It" button to change either movie or meal. Change your mind? Select the Restart button to change the selections!
